## REMOVED Requirements

### Requirement: Survey cards on landing page
**Reason**: The landing page no longer displays survey cards. The open-source product landing page focuses on features, comparison, and CTAs rather than listing individual surveys. Survey cards remain available on the `/editor/` dashboard for authenticated users.
**Migration**: Survey browsing is available via the editor dashboard. Public survey discovery may be added later via a dedicated `/surveys/` browse page.

### Requirement: Survey card content
**Reason**: Removed along with survey cards on landing page.
**Migration**: Survey card rendering is retained in the editor dashboard templates only.

### Requirement: Survey card response count
**Reason**: Removed along with survey cards on landing page.
**Migration**: Response counts are available in the editor dashboard.

### Requirement: Survey cards ordering
**Reason**: Removed along with survey cards on landing page.
**Migration**: Ordering logic is retained for the editor dashboard survey list.

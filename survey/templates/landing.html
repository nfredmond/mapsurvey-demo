How it works

Turn community input into mapped advocacy evidence.

  1. Launch a map survey. Residents draw routes, drop pins, outline zones, and answer context questions on mobile or desktop.
  2. Review responses. Staff validate submissions, tag themes, inspect maps, and track completion funnels in the editor dashboard.
  3. Export for action. Download GeoJSON and CSV for council briefings, SANDAG conversations, grant narratives, and public reports.
